## Competitive Landscape

The local search space is split across three types of competitors:

- Biomedical service vendors: companies offering equipment repair, maintenance, installation and technical support.
- Calibration and metrology providers: labs and technical organizations focused on measurement traceability and calibration services.
- Regulatory and radiological safety providers: consultants or institutions supporting radiation safety, permits, documentation and compliance.

The site should compete by being more specific than broad equipment vendors: each service page must clearly mention equipment types, industries served, documentation delivered, response expectations and Panama service coverage.
